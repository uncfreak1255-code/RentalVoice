## App Store Review Notes

**Demo Account:**
The app includes a built-in Demo Mode. When launched without connecting a property management system, the app displays sample conversations with realistic guest messages, AI-generated draft replies, and calendar data. No login or API key is needed to explore all features.

**How to test in Demo Mode:**
1. Launch the app
2. Skip the onboarding (or don't connect a PMS)
3. The Inbox will show 5 sample conversations with AI draft replies
4. Tap any conversation to see the full chat thread
5. Tap "Send Draft" to see the send flow (messages stay local in demo mode)
6. Check the Calendar tab for sample bookings
7. Explore Settings for all configuration options

**What the app does:**
Rental Voice helps vacation rental property managers respond to guest messages faster using AI. It connects to property management systems (like Hostaway) to fetch real conversations, then uses AI (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic — user's choice of provider) to draft contextually-aware replies.

**Network access:**
The app requires internet to connect to the user's property management system API and to generate AI drafts. In demo mode, no network calls are made.
