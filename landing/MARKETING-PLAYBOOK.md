## 🏘️ Local Host Outreach Strategy

### Step 1: Find Hosts in Your Area

| Source | How to Find Them |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Airbnb search** | Search your city, filter to Superhosts with 3+ listings. Note their names. |
| **VRBO/Booking.com** | Same — find multi-property hosts in your market |
| **Property manager directories** | VRMA.org, local STR associations |
| **Facebook groups** | "[Your City] Airbnb Hosts", "[Your City] STR Community" |
| **Hostaway community** | Forums, Facebook group, LinkedIn posts |
| **Local STR meetups** | Meetup.com, Eventbrite — these are gold for relationships |
| **Google Maps** | Search "vacation rental management [your city]" |

### Step 2: Personalize Every Message

Before reaching out, check their listing. Mention:
- Their property name or location
- Something specific about their listing (design, amenities)
- A shared challenge (seasonality, noise complaints, late check-ins)

**Never spam.** You're a fellow host sharing something you built. That authenticity is your biggest advantage.

### Step 3: Weekly Outreach Cadence

| Week | Activity | Volume |
|------|----------|--------|
| **Week 1** | Personal emails to 20 local hosts | 20 emails |
| **Week 2** | Follow up non-responders + 15 new hosts | 25 emails |
| **Week 3** | Post in 5 Facebook groups + 10 new hosts | 5 posts + 10 emails |
| **Week 4** | Attend 1 local meetup + LinkedIn posts | 1 event + 3 posts |

**Goal:** 10 beta users by end of month 1.
